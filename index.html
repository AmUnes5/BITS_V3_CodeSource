Who are we ?

BITS was Established in 2004 , in Casablanca - Morocco by a group of experts in order to exploit 20 years of experience in a new era of electronic banking at an international level.

Our mission

As a service and engineering company, we are committed to offer the best experience in electronic payment methods, based on trust and on perceived value for clients, shareholders and employees.

Our duty

We provide a complete suite of software products and services for processing electronic transactions and managing their entire life cyle and value chain.

Our vision
