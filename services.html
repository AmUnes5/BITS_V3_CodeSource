EMV Migration

BITS has the right resources and consultants to support the bank EMV migration. With past EMV experience, the lead consultant can bring the technology knowledge, complex payment project management, full understanding of EMV impact on systems and organizations, acquaintance with banking environment, departments, regulation and organization, communication skills at all levels to help :

  • Spread awareness of EMV technology requirement, impact, opportunity to all internal stakeholders.
  • Facilitate decision making within the organization by pointing out the right decision to be made by the right stakeholder and pursuing the right chain of decision.
  • Navigate within the bank and its vendor to bring concensus in planning and delivery.
  • Interface and have the right dialogue / terminology with various external stakeholder such as VISA-MasterCARD (implementation and certification projects), CMS provider (implementation of various CMS systems), Card issuance (personalization projects).
  • Interface and have the right dialogue / terminology with various internal stakeholders. Past IT and banking experience make the consultant capable of handling IT as well as business talk with internal stakeholders.

Testing Services

BITS has detailed testing scripts on major card activity: issuing, acquiring, ATM, POS. BITS offer onsite testing service by providing :

  • Test book : comprehensive test cases and scenarios for the scope of the project.
  • Test Resources : experts who have broad payment experience and faced scenarios and cases to be solved, avoided.
  • Test Tools : simulators, injectors, replay tools, test repository.
  • Performance test tools for ATM/POS/Transaction : measure the current server capacity in term of number of ATMs/POSs/Transaction that can be handled.

BITS School

BITS provides a multi-sessions training for professionals :

  • General Overview of electronic payments.
  • NDC & Diebold Protocol : Protocol for exchange between ATM and Switches.
  • ISO 8583 Message Protocol : Protocol for exchange between ATM and Switches.
  • EMV Specification and Standard : New chip cards EMV requirements and standards.
  • HSM and security : Security cryptography for EFT applications including HSM module.
  • Starndard PCI-DSS : Understanding the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard.
  • Fraud Management : Fraud Management techniques : Online/BackOffice fraud, Issuer/acquirer Fraud.
  • Chargebacks and Claims Management : Include Claim following, decision making tools, transactions adjustments...
  • According to your needs, we can also propose tailored training planning.
